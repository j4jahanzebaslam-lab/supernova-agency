✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything TikTok Live creators are searching for — answered.

🔒 Do I have to give you my TikTok password or log-in details?

Never. We will never ask for your password, email log-ins, or two-factor authentication codes.

How it works: Onboarding happens completely inside the TikTok app via an official TikTok In-App Network Invitation. You maintain 100% control, ownership, and security over your account at all times.
🔒 Is my personal data and identity secure?

Yes. Your personal information remains strictly protected under global privacy standards (GDPR in the UK, APPI in Japan, PDPA in Malaysia, and US state privacy laws).

Your tax and payout details are managed directly by TikTok—we never see or store your financial banking credentials.
💰 Will the agency take a cut from my LIVE earnings or Diamond gifts?

No. You keep 100% of your earnings and gifts.

The Myth: Creators often worry agencies will take 20% to 50% of their revenue.
The Reality: Official TikTok LIVE Creator Networks are compensated directly by TikTok through platform bonuses for growing creators. If an agency asks for a percentage of your gift earnings, they are violating official TikTok rules.
💰 How do I get paid? Does my money go through the agency first?

No, you are paid directly by TikTok. You withdraw your earnings straight from your TikTok app balance to your connected bank account or payment processor (e.g., PayPal, local bank transfer).

Your money never touches our accounts, guaranteeing zero payout delays.
📄 Am I trapped in a long-term contract if I join?

No. You can leave at any time with a simple 30-day notice.

Complete Flexibility: We don't believe in locking creators into long, restrictive legal traps. If you ever feel our network is no longer a good fit, you can submit a 30-day notice to unlink directly via TikTok's official backstage center. We earn your trust through performance, not enforced contracts.
📄 Why should I join a network instead of streaming alone?

Streaming alone means facing algorithm shifts, false bans, and technical issues by yourself. When you join our network, you keep 100% of your money while getting access to agency-exclusive perks:

  • 100% Earnings
🚀 Network Creator
  • Fast-track issue resolution with TikTok reps
  • 1-on-1 coaching & stream optimization
  • LIVE Studio access & exclusive contests

⚠️ What happens if I get falsely reported or flagged?

If you receive a false report:

  • Don't panic — appeals are your right
  • Document all evidence and communications
  • Submit a detailed appeal through TikTok's support
  • Contact your agency or partners for support
We have a dedicated team to help you through appeals. Having a strong appeal strategy can save your account.
📈 How do I increase my diamond revenue?

Boost your diamond earnings by:

  • Creating high-quality, entertaining streams
  • Engaging directly with gifters and supporters
